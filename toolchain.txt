Full Static Analysis Toolchain (Summer 2019)

The toolchain created by Zach Crenshaw in Spring 2019 has been reduced to:
    running run.py for a module (i.e. events-L1) to turn the raw data to CSV format
    runniing plotGrade.py to transform the data to usable graphs

Given that there exists a directory for a module with the following path structure:
/[module]
.    /csv
.    .   /4     (a folder only for each grade present in the metadata CSV)
.    .   /5
.    .   /6
.    .   /7
.    .   /8
.    .   /aggregated
.    /graphs
.    .   /pngs
.    /json_files_by_studio
.   metadata-[module].csv

and the required python3 modules are installed, the following example should work when typed into the command line

run.py ./[module]/metadata-[module].csv
plotGrade.py ./[module]

If you were to do it with an actual module (i.e. copying and pasting the following lines), it would look like:

run.py ./events-L1/metadata-events-L1.csv
plotGrade.py ./events-L1
